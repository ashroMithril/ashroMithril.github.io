Context & Role

Led the technology shift from NLP to LLM-based workflow identification to dramatically improve conversation quality and ticket deflection rates. This was a platform-wide transformation affecting all customer bots.

Problem & Opportunity

Around 40%+ undeflected tickets were going to fallback due to poor intent identification, inability to handle context switching, and irrelevant small talk responses.

Results & Impact

Increased deflection rate to 80%+ from existing 60%. Today, 100% of platform bots run on Orchestrator LLM - both old bots migrated from NLP and all new bots built on this foundation.

Problem & Opportunity

Over 50% of support conversations were happening in email. Companies with 100 agents and 50k monthly tickets ended up with 500 emails per agent, leading to delayed responses and poor customer experience.

Solution

Built MVP in one month: KB-based automation tackling huge multi-intent emails, noise clearing, segmenting, multi-thread responses, API-based responses, sentiment analysis, and OCR for attachments.

Results & Impact

Achieved 80%+ resolution rate within 1 week of launch. Secured first million-dollar deals. Feature set expanded to include multi-thread handling, multi-intent processing, and seamless agent handoffs.
